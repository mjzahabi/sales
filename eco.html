Spray-and-Pray Marketing
Sending campaigns to everyone instead of the right person at the right moment
Blanket email blasts, mass direct mail, and conquest ads bought on demographic targeting reach a fraction of the people at the right moment. The cost isn't just the media — it's the diminishing returns on every campaign that follows.
AMP triggers only when a customer crosses a behavioral threshold — VDP visits, trade lookup, payment build — so every dollar of marketing spend reaches someone who has already shown intent, not someone who might be interested someday.

The average salesperson spends 30-40% of their follow-up time on leads that have gone cold or were never warm to begin with.
